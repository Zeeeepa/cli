# FAQ

## What is TestDriver?

TestDriver is a comprehensive testing tool designed to streamline the process of software testing. It provides a suite of features to automate, manage, and analyze tests effectively.

## How do I install TestDriver?

To install TestDriver, follow these steps:
1. Download the installer from the official website.
2. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
3. Configure the tool according to your requirements.

## What platforms does TestDriver support?

TestDriver supports multiple platforms, including Windows, macOS, and Linux.

## Can I integrate TestDriver with other tools?

Yes, TestDriver offers integration capabilities with various tools such as CI/CD pipelines, bug tracking systems, and version control systems.

## Where can I find documentation for TestDriver?

You can find detailed documentation on the official TestDriver website under the "Documentation" section.

## How do I report issues or request features?

To report issues or request features, visit the "Support" section on the official TestDriver website and submit your feedback.

## Is there a community for TestDriver users?

Yes, there is an active community of TestDriver users. You can join forums, participate in discussions, and share your experiences.

## Does TestDriver offer a free trial?

Yes, TestDriver provides a free trial for new users. You can sign up on the official website to access the trial version.

## How do I contact TestDriver support?

You can contact TestDriver support through the "Contact Us" page on the official website or via email at support@testdriver.com.
